About VAIEP

The Vietnam AI for Education Program, commonly known as VAIEP, was established with the ambitious objective of infusing the essence, knowledge, and practical skills of AI tool utilization into educational institutions. This initiative targets teachers and students as primary beneficiaries, aiming to cultivate a generation adept not only in navigating the future but also in actively shaping it.

Pioneered by educator Nguyen Thuy Uyen Phuong, the author of 'Hello AI: Learn and Play with Artificial Intelligence', the program represents a collaborative effort involving dedicated colleagues and visionaries passionate about securing a progressive future for Vietnam's youth in the AI era."

Future Learning Pioneers Scholarship

The scholarship aims to support educators across all levels in Vietnam in enhancing their teaching capabilities through the use of Artificial Intelligence (AI). Scholarship recipients will be trained to become "Future Learning Pioneers" – innovators in education who embrace new methods and tools of the AI era, contributing to improving the quality of education and sparking creativity in students.

The Vietnam AI for Education Program (VAIEP) is a non-profit initiative designed to support Vietnam's education system in adapting to the transformative advancements of the AI era. By fostering the adoption of innovative, AI-driven teaching methods, VAIEP seeks to modernize educational practices and enhance learning outcomes.

Founded by educator Nguyễn Thúy Uyên Phương and managed by FAROS Education & Consulting, the program is generously funded by the U.S. government in commemoration of the 30th anniversary of Vietnam-U.S. diplomatic relations.
